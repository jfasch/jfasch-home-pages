Use Cases¶

Trainer¶

  • Prepares trainings based upon existing material.

  • Navigates the audience through the training, trying to not deviate from the agenda too much.

  • After the training, provides the audience with a writeup of what has happened, consisting of

    • A collection of questions asked and answered

    • A list of covered topics, in case the training itself deviates from the agenda (likely)

    • A number of “See also” links to other topics from the base material that have not been covered, or to further information on the Internet.

  • Use Case: Custom Training, Subtract Known
