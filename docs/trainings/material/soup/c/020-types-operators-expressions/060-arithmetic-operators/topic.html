Arithmetic Operators (2)¶

Precedence rules

  • Multiplication, division, modulo precede addition, subtraction

  • Operators with same precedence are associated from left to right

(30 / 2) + 1

precedence

1 + 30 / 2

precedence

1 - 2 + 3

left-associative
