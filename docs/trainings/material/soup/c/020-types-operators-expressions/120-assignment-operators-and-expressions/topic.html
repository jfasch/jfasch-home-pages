Assignment with Calculation#

Combined Operators#

The long way

i = i + 2;
arr[j] = arr[j] + 2;

The short way

i += 2;
arr[j] += 2;

  • Less writing (⟶ confusion)

  • Expression evaluated only once ⟶ performance

  • Applies to +, -, *, /, %, <<, >>, &, ^, |
