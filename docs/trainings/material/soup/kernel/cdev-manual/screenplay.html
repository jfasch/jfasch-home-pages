Major and Minor Numbers¶

  • register_chrdev_region() in init(), and unregister_chrdev_region() in exit()

    • Caveat: kernel development just happens. The following methods predate the cdev API; still used by old drivers.

      • Not register_chrdev() - that does cdev_add() implicitly

      • Not unregister_chrdev() - that leaves the major number registered, at least.

    • Hardcode major and minor numbers to (1,1) initially ⟶ error in logs (KERN_ERR)

    • Emphasize on error handling (return -EINVAL)

    • Take (42,1), finally works

      • See how major shows up in /proc/devices
