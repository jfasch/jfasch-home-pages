Pricing, Organizational#

Course Modes#

Onsite/Online#

Courses can be delivered in the following formats:

  • Onsite for a single customer organization. Travel expenses are charged individually (usually I travel from Graz/Austria).

  • Virtual/online for a single customer organization [1]. Verified platforms are

    • Zoom

    • Microsoft Teams

    • BigBlueButton

Delivery/Material#

See also

  • Complete Slide Material

  • Log Of Past Courses

All material is available online from this site. Attendees are left with personalized writeups of their particular event as it happened.

Pricing#

  • The price for one course day is €1300.

  • One day is typically around 8.5 hours (including breaks).

  • Travel expenses, if any, are charged additionally (usually I travel from Graz/Austria)

Individual#

See also

  • Course Descriptions

  • Log Of Past Courses

  • Myself: Contact, Impressum, …

Nearly all courses that I hold are composed individually. Course descriptions should be seen as blueprints for courses that are rarely delivered in exactly that form. Please browse the course log to see what a typical course looks like.

Please come in contact with me, describing your wishes. Cite course descriptions that you read here, list topics that you want included in a training. The information need not be structured - just throw it at me.

I’ll then compose a training for you; this need not necessarily match a course description, but can be composed from the huge amount of course material.
