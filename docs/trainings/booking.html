How To Book¶

  • training@faschingbauer.co.at

  • About myself

  • Course descriptions

  • All course material (slides?)

Please send an email training@faschingbauer.co.at, describing your wishes. Cite course descriptions that you read here, list topics that you want included in a training. The information need not be structured - just throw it at me (or simply call me), and lets get in contact.

We’ll then compose a training for you; this need not necessarily match a course description, but can be composed from the huge amount of course material. (Literally all courses that I give are customer-specific; see here for a list)

All courses can be booked in an online setting [1] too; verified platforms are

  • Zoom

  • Microsoft Teams
